====== Religion ======
Created Monday 22 January 2024

Religions by race:
	* Bear - Worships the Gods of Order and Nature
	* Cat - Worships the gods of Earth and Divine Magic
	* Elf - Worships the Greater Divine Gods
	* Human - Are not unified over all in faith. But most will deny the GOD of the Empire if questioned. 
	* Tonberry - Worships the Gods of White and Black Magic
